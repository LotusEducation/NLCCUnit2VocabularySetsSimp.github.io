Compound Word Components


  1. 名（名字） míng N: name
  1. 字（名字） zì N: word; character
  1. 好（你好） hǎo Adj: good; fine; nice (你好 literally means "you good")
  1. 再（再见） zài Adv: again; once more
  1. 见（再见） jiàn V: meet; see
  1. 很（很高兴认识你） hěn Adv: very
  1. 高（高兴） gāo Adj: tall; high; of a high level/degree (高兴 means happy)
  1. 兴（高兴） xìng Ｎ: excitement; mood or desire to do sth; interest
  1. 认（认识） rèn V: recognize; know
  1. 识（认识） shí V: to know, understand (In Taiwan it is pronounced shì）
  1. 什（什么） shén N: what
  1. 么（什么） me Suffix: used in interogatives
  1. 贵（贵姓） guì your (courteous); highly valued; expensive (Note: 贵姓 is a very polite way to say "your last name". It's used when asking for someone's last name.)
  1. 请（请问） qǐng please; V: ask, request
  1. 问（请问） wèn V: ask; inquire
